Fundamental of Fluid Dynamics for the spread of COVID-19

In this article we sum up how the principle of fluid dynamics holds the response for the spread of COVID-19. Understanding the ways by which the infection is being transmitted can educate general wellbeing mediations to limit the danger of outspread of COVID-19. This information may also enlighten the public authority arrangements to lessen the spread of future pandemic.
